Privacy Policy

This application uses TikTok’s Content Posting API to upload videos created by the user. We do not collect, store, or share personal data beyond what is required to authenticate with TikTok’s API.

Information we handle

  • Access tokens – required to publish videos on TikTok. These are stored securely in n8n credentials.
  • Video files – generated by the workflow and uploaded directly to TikTok.

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties. Users may revoke access at any time via their TikTok account settings.
